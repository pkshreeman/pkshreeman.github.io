An alert reported that top-ranked U.I.A. squad aboard the scouting starship named Rainbow Phoenix has gone missing. Galactic Senator Starwhisperer has been alerted of the squad’s disappearance and is concerned that all contact has been lost. General “Rex” Tyrannosaur is in contact with the crew of the Midnight Horizon to give aid to any survivors of the Rainbow Phoenix once it’s found. The success of this recovery mission could prove to be an enormous career for Captain Stigmata and his squad. Little do they know how this fateful mission will impact their legacy throughout the vast universe.
