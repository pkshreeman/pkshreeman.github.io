How can I partake into this wonderful practice?

What you would do is to "adopt" a cow in our care, by purchasing a share in the herd. The first step is to make one-time payment per share. Once you purhcased the share, you have a monthly boarding fee for feeding and care of your ladies. Each Share is equivalent to one half gallon jar of cow milk.
